DON'T BREATHE

7.5

Storyline

He's blind and rich -- the perfect target for robbery. But the tables turn when three teen thieves find themselves in a lethal game of cat and mouse.

Trivia

  1. Stephen Lang wore contact lenses that greatly restricted his vision, particularly in low light. The other actors, in the scene taking place in the dark, wore lenses that made them look like they had dilated pupils but also greatly restricted their vision.
  2. Ironically, the street where the blind man lives is called "Buena Vista," which is translated to mean "Good View" in Spanish.
  3. Was number #1 in the U.S. Box Office for the first two weeks of release and stayed in the top 10 for six weeks.
  4. This film was originally titled, "A Man in the Dark." Director Fede Alvarez plays the viola in the soundtrack of the film.
