SOCIAL DILEMMA

7.5

Storyline

This documentary-drama hybrid explores the dangerous human impact of social networking, with tech experts sounding the alarm on their own creations.

Trivia

  1. Premiered at the 2020 Sundance Film Festival.
  2. Jeff Orlowski was a heavy Facebook user before making the film. He's not now.
  3. Vincent Kartheiser, who features in the film-within-the-film, ironically has no connections with social networks in any shape or form. He and his wife Alexis Bledel eschew all the trappings of Hollywood in favor of a simple living lifestyle
  4. One of the subjects, Tristan Harris, is a college friend of the director, Jeff Orlowski.

Details

  • Genre: Documentary,Drama
  • Director: Jeff Orlowski
  • Writers: Orlowski,Davis Coombe,Vickie Curtis.
  • Stars: Skyler Gisondo,Kara Hayward,Vincent Kartheiser
