Storyline

Time travelers arrive from 2051 to deliver an urgent message: 30 years in the future mankind is losing a war against deadly alien species.The only hope for survival is for soldiers and civillians to be transported to the future and join the fight.Determined to save the world for his daughter, Dan Forester teams up with a brilliant scientist and his estranged father to rewrite the planets fate

Trivia

  1. During the film, when the military man talks about how the time travel works, he says 'it's like putting chicken wire together with bubble gum'. Which is a clear reference to a scene in the time travel movie, 'Bill and Ted's Excellent Adventure'.
  2. "In memory of Dave Parra", credited in this film as 'Draftee Angel'.
  3. This film was made by Paramount Pictures for a theatrical release but due to the Covid-19 pandemic, Paramount sold the film to Amazon Prime Video for $200 million.
  4. Shot over a period of four months, including location filming in Iceland.
