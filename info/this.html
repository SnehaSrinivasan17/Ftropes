Storyline

Three siblings, go through unique personal struggles at different intervals of life as they try to find happiness and get over a tragedy in their past

Trivia

  1. Sterling K. Brown's Golden Globe award for Best Actor in this drama TV series made him the first black man to win this category in the ceremony's 75-year history
  2. Almost completely broke when she landed the role of Kate on the series, Chrissy Metz only had 81 cents in her bank account when she auditioned for the series
  3. Mandy Moore is the youngest actor in the main cast despite playing the mother of Randall, Kate and Kevin.
  4. When Rebecca and Randall say to each other "I love you", "I love you more", "I love you most", this is a reference to Tangled (2010), in which Mandy Moore starred
