ALICE IN BORDERLAND

7.5

Storyline

An aimless gamer and his two friends find themselves in a parallel Tokyo, where they're forced to compete in a series of sadistic games to survive.

Trivia

  1. This is a retelling of Lewis Carroll's "Alice in Wonderland" story, with the characters' names reflecting their role in the story (Ryohei Arisu is named after Alice, Yuzuha Usagi is the White Rabbit, etc).
  2. Usagi is the Japanese word for rabbit. So the character Yuzuha Usagi represents the rabbit.
  3. Producers filmed the Shibuya crossing scenes 100 kilometers away in Ashikaga
